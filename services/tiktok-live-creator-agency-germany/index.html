TikTok LIVE Creator Agency Germany

Who This Is For

  • TikTok LIVE creators in Germany and DACH who want structured growth support.
  • Streamers who need content feedback, workshop support, analytics, and monetization guidance.
  • Brands that want to work with creators and live-streaming talent in a more systematic way.
  • Platform, entertainment, gaming, beauty, lifestyle, music, and creator-economy teams building creator-led campaigns.

What PLUSWEG Does

  • Talent identification and creator onboarding.
  • Creator management and growth strategy.
  • TikTok LIVE format development.
  • Short-form content strategy for creator discovery.
  • Community engagement planning.

Case-Study Proof

The strongest proof point is the TikTok Live Germany case study. In direct cooperation with TikTok, PLUSWEG worked on identifying high-potential creators, onboarding them to the platform, educating and training them, and increasing on-platform revenue and reach.

Reported 24-month results:

  • $8.9M total revenue across the creator portfolio.
  • 687M total viewers reached.
  • 7.43M converted followers.
  • 3.2M paying users.
  • 54% average month-to-month growth in the first six months.
  • 16% sustained average month-to-month growth across the full contract period.
