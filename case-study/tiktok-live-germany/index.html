TikTok Live Germany Case Study

In direct cooperation with TikTok, PLUSWEG was tasked with identifying high-potential creators, onboarding them to the platform, educating and training them to maximize growth, and increasing on-platform revenue and reach.

Challenge

TikTok needed a trusted partner in the DACH region to scale the TikTok LIVE ecosystem from the ground up. The live-streaming vertical was still early in German-speaking markets, and many creators had little experience with live formats. The work required creator onboarding, category-specific playbooks, workshops, agency training, and support for turning engagement into sustainable revenue.

Approach

PLUSWEG prioritized quality over quantity in creator management. Instead of only signing a large roster, the team built a focused creator development program covering community engagement, short-form content strategy, visual brand identity, long-form content planning, analytics, and monetization.

The work covered genres including gaming, lifestyle, beauty, music, and entertainment. PLUSWEG also contributed to the broader ecosystem through workshops at official TikTok events, agency training, and panel talks around gaming, live content, and creator monetization.

Results

  • $8.9M total revenue across the creator portfolio over 24 months.
  • 687M total viewers reached.
  • 7.43M converted followers.
  • 3.2M paying users.
  • 54% average month-to-month growth in the first six months.
  • 16% sustained average month-to-month growth across the full contract period.

Services Delivered

  • Talent management.
  • Talent acquisition.
  • Social media strategy.
  • Growth strategy.
  • Media training and education.
  • Brand awareness and outreach.
  • Industry workshops and panels.
  • Agency training.

Key Takeaways

  • Quality over quantity in creator management can lead to higher per-creator revenue.
  • A focused regional approach in DACH creates a strong base for controlled international expansion.
  • Direct platform cooperation enables deeper integration and measurable commercial outcomes.
  • Industry presence through workshops and panel talks accelerates ecosystem credibility and partner trust.
